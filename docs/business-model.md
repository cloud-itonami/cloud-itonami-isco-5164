# Business Model: Independent Pet Care Practice

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-5164`
- ISCO-08: `5164`
- Occupation: Pet Groomers and Animal Care Workers
- Social impact: local-jobs, animal-welfare, small-business-viability

## Customer

- pet owners
- shelters
- boarding facilities

## Offer

- grooming support
- animal-health record keeping
- appointment scheduling
- reporting

## Revenue

- per-session fee
- monthly practice platform fee
- reporting package

## Trust Controls

- robot never left unsupervised with an anxious/aggressive animal
- any incident triggers immediate human escalation, cannot be suppressed
- health records are auditable, not editable
